<h5>Gold</h5>
<div>
    <p class="lead">
        You have a Gold Account. 
        As a Guest you have the following search restrictions.
    </p>
    <ol>
        <li>
            Searches: 
            The first 20 records returned from a search are billed at 4 cents per lead.
            Additional records in the search are billed at 5 cents per lead.
        </li>
        <li>
            Monthly Limit: 
            Your account is limited to receive 1500 leads in a calendar month.</li>
        <li>
            Annual Limit: 
            Your account is limited to receive 10000 leads in a calendar year.</li>
    </ol>
    <p>
        Billing Types: 
    </p>
    <ul>
        <li>Monthly - $20 per month</li>
        <li>Annual - $225 per year.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>
        Discounts: <br>
        On top pf your current pricing, you are eligible to purchase State and/or County level subscriptions
    </p>
    <ul>
        <li>State Discount - 15% discount applied to eligible records</li>
        <li>County Discount - 10% discount applied to eligible records</li>
    </ul>
